STUDY IN USA

Thinking about studying in the USA from Bangladesh and need help selecting a program or the right university? MASJT can guide you! Process your application through trained consultants and graduates with experience in U.S. education.

Why Study in USA

Studying in the United States offers numerous advantages, including access to top-ranked universities, innovative teaching, and a rich cultural experience. Here are a few reasons why studying in the U.S. is a great choice:

World-Class Education

The U.S. is home to some of the best universities and colleges globally. Institutions like Harvard, MIT, and Stanford consistently rank among the top in the world, offering cutting-edge programs and research opportunities.

Diverse Academic Programs

Whether you're interested in science, engineering, arts, business, or law, the U.S. offers a wide variety of academic programs. With flexible curricula, you can tailor your studies to match your career goals.

Research and Innovation

The U.S. leads in global research and technological innovations. By studying here, you can access state-of-the-art labs, resources, and opportunities to participate in groundbreaking research.

Improved Career Prospects

U.S. degrees are globally recognized, and many international companies highly value graduates from U.S. institutions. Additionally, many universities have strong connections with industry professionals, which can help you land internships and job opportunities.

Work Opportunities for Students

As an international student in the U.S., you can work part-time during your studies (up to 20 hours per week during the academic year) and full-time during breaks, helping to support yourself financially and gain work experience.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Tier 4 Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

The main intake period is in August/September (Fall intake). Some universities may also offer January (Spring) intake.

English Language Requirements

Most universities require a TOEFL or IELTS score. For undergraduate programs, an IELTS score of 6.0 to 7.0 is typically required, while postgraduate programs may require 6.5 to 8.0.

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA 4.75 to 5.0 in HSC / Min 2C in A Level to 3A* in A Level.
  • Few Universities require Math course in 12th Grade for Business and Science programs.

Postgraduate – Masters

  • CGPA 2.50+
  • GRE or GMAT is not required for Business, Science, and Engineering programs.
  • For MBA programs at some Universities, students need to have work experience along with GMAT score.

Each institution sets its own entry requirements, and you will find that they vary between universities. Your application will normally be assessed on your academic level of qualification. To make sure you meet all the admissions criteria for the course(s) you wish to apply for, contact the international office of the institutions you are applying to and check.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an F-1 student visa, you will need to meet the following requirements:

  • I-20 Form: Issued by your U.S. university after acceptance.
  • Visa Fees: The SEVIS fee is approximately $350, and the visa application fee is around $160.
  • Visa Interview: Typically required at the U.S. embassy in Dhaka.

Financial Requirements for F-1 Visa:

You must demonstrate that you can pay for your tuition and living expenses. Typically, this involves showing bank statements, affidavits of support, or proof of financial aid. The required amount varies by university but is generally around:

  • Tuition Fees: $15,000 – $50,000 per year (depending on the university and program).
  • Living Expenses: Estimated at $10,000 – $20,000 per year, depending on the location.

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Regular Processing: 10–20 working days.
  • Expedited (Premium): Approximately 7 working days.
