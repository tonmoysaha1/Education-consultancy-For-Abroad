STUDY IN NETHERLANDS

Are you planning to study in the Netherlands from Bangladesh? MASJT is here to assist you in finding the perfect program and university to pursue your academic goals. With expert guidance, we ensure a seamless application process for studying in one of the most innovative and welcoming countries in Europe.

Why Study in NETHERLANDS

The Netherlands is a leading destination for international students due to its globally recognized education system, diverse English-taught programs, and high quality of life. Here’s why the Netherlands is an excellent choice:

World-Class Education

Dutch universities are renowned for their academic excellence, particularly in fields like engineering, technology, business, and social sciences. Institutions like Delft University of Technology (TU Delft), University of Amsterdam, and Erasmus University Rotterdam are ranked among the top globally.

English-Taught Programs

The Netherlands offers over 2,100 programs taught entirely in English, making it an attractive option for international students who may not speak Dutch.

Innovative Learning Approach

Dutch universities emphasize interactive learning, problem-solving, and collaboration, preparing students for practical challenges in their careers.

Affordable Tuition Fees

Tuition fees in the Netherlands are competitive compared to other European countries. There are also numerous scholarships available for international students.

Post-Study Work Opportunities

International students can apply for the Orientation Year Visa, allowing them to stay in the Netherlands for one year after graduation to find employment.

Multicultural Environment

As a multicultural and progressive country, the Netherlands welcomes students from all over the world, providing a rich cultural and social experience.

Strategic Location in Europe

The Netherlands is centrally located in Europe, offering easy access to major cities like Paris, London, and Berlin for travel and networking opportunities.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Proof of accommodation in the Netherlands
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

Netherlands universities generally have two main intakes:

  • September (Main Intake): Most programs
  • February (Secondary Intake): Limited programs

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (6.0–7.0) or TOEFL iBT (80–100).
  • Dutch-Taught Programs: NT2-II or equivalent for Dutch language proficiency.

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • A minimum CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an Netherlands Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Dutch university.
  • Submit proof of financial sufficiency and health insurance.
  • Apply for an MVV visa (provisional residence permit).
  • Attend a visa appointment at the Dutch Embassy or Consulate.
  • Approximately €210

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • EU/EEA Students: €2,500–€4,000 per year.
  • Non-EU Students: €6,000–€20,000 per year (varies by program and institution).
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: €800–€1,200 (includes accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: €300–€600 per month
  • Private apartments: €800–€1200 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Opportunities:

After graduating, international students can apply for the Orientation Year Visa, allowing them to stay in the Netherlands for up to one year to look for a job. Many graduates find opportunities in fields such as technology, business, and logistics.
