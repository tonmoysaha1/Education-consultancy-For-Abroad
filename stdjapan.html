Thinking about studying in JAPAN from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN JAPAN

Are you considering studying in Japan from Bangladesh? MASJT can help you navigate the process of choosing the right program and university in one of the world’s most innovative and culturally rich countries. With our guidance, achieve your dream of a quality education in Japan.

Why Study in JAPAN

Japan is a top destination for international students due to its advanced education system, technological prowess, and unique cultural experience. Here are the reasons to choose Japan for your studies:

World-Class Education

Japan is home to prestigious institutions like The University of Tokyo, Kyoto University, and Osaka University, known for their excellence in engineering, technology, and the humanities.

Innovative Learning and Research

Japan’s universities emphasize cutting-edge research and innovation, making it an ideal destination for students in science, technology, and engineering.

Diverse English-Taught Programs

Many universities in Japan now offer programs taught in English, allowing international students to study without requiring advanced Japanese proficiency.

Affordable Tuition Fees

Japan offers comparatively low tuition fees, and numerous scholarship programs, such as the MEXT Scholarship, cover tuition, living expenses, and airfare.

Global Career Opportunities

Japan’s strong economy and ties to global industries provide excellent job opportunities for international graduates in fields like technology, automotive, and finance.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Admission letter from a Japanese university
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

Japanese universities generally have two main intakes:

  • April (Main Intake): Most universities
  • October (Secondary Intake): Available for some programs

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90).
  • Japanese-Taught Programs: JLPT N2 or N1 level proficiency.

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • A minimum CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for a Japanese Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Japanese university.
  • Receive the Certificate of Eligibility (COE) from your institution.
  • Submit the visa application at the Japanese Embassy or Consulate in your country.
  • Approximately $30–$50

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: $5,000–$8,000 per year.
  • Postgraduate Programs: $7,000–$10,000 per year.
  • National Universities: Lower tuition fees compared to private institutions
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: $700–$1,200 (includes accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: $200–$500 per month
  • Private apartments: $400–$800 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Opportunities:

International students can apply for a Work Visa after graduation. Japan actively recruits international talent in sectors such as engineering, IT, and healthcare, making it a promising place to start a career.
