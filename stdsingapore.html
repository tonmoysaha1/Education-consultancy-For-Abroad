Thinking about studying in SINGAPORE from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN SINGAPORE

Are you considering studying in Singapore from Bangladesh? MASJT can guide you in selecting the ideal program and university to suit your academic and career ambitions. Work with our expert consultants and graduates who understand Singapore’s dynamic education system.

Why Study in SINGAPORE

Singapore is a global education hub, offering world-class universities, a multicultural environment, and excellent career prospects. Here are the top reasons to choose Singapore for your studies:

World-Class Universities

Singapore is home to globally recognized institutions such as National University of Singapore (NUS) and Nanyang Technological University (NTU), both ranked among the top universities worldwide.

Quality Education System

Singapore’s education system emphasizes innovation, practical learning, and academic excellence, ensuring that students are well-prepared for the global job market.

Strategic Location

Located in the heart of Asia, Singapore offers a gateway to booming economies and serves as a hub for multinational companies.

Career Opportunities

Singapore’s strong economy and status as a business hub create ample job opportunities for graduates in industries such as finance, technology, and engineering.

Multicultural Environment

Singapore is a melting pot of cultures, providing a unique and enriching experience for students from diverse backgrounds.

Safe and Modern City

With low crime rates, clean surroundings, and efficient public transport, Singapore is one of the safest and most modern cities in the world.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Tier 4 Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

  • August (Main Intake): Most universities
  • January (Secondary Intake): Available for select programs

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (6.0–7.0) or TOEFL iBT (80–100)

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA 4.75 to 5.0 in HSC / Min 2C in A Level to 3A* in A Level.
  • Few Universities require Math course in 12th Grade for Business and Science programs.

Postgraduate – Masters

  • CGPA 2.50+
  • GRE or GMAT is not required for Business, Science, and Engineering programs.
  • For MBA programs at some Universities, students need to have work experience along with GMAT score.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an Singapore Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized institution in Singapore.
  • Submit your Student Pass application via the SOLAR+ system provided by the Immigration & Checkpoints Authority (ICA).
  • Attend a medical examination and submit required documents.
  • Collect your Student Pass upon arrival in Singapore.
  • Application fee: SGD 30–SGD 60
  • Issuance fee: SGD 60

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: SGD 10,000–SGD 20,000 per year (for international students).
  • Postgraduate Programs: SGD 20,000–SGD 40,000 per year (varies by program and university).
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: SGD 1,000–SGD 2,500 (includes accommodation, food, transport, and other expenses).

Accommodation Options:

  • On-campus housing: SGD 400–SGD 800 per month
  • Private apartments: SGD 800–SGD 1,500 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Opportunities:

After graduating, international students can apply for a Long-Term Visit Pass (LTVP) or a Work Pass to stay in Singapore and find a job. The city’s strong economy and global connections make it an ideal place to start a career.
