Study in SOUTH KOREA

Thinking about studying in SOUTH KOREA from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN SOUTH KOREA

Dreaming of studying in South Korea from Bangladesh? MASJT can guide you through selecting the perfect program and university, ensuring a seamless application process. South Korea’s advanced education system and dynamic culture make it an ideal destination for international students.

Why Study in SOUTH KOREA

South Korea is a global education hub, renowned for its academic excellence, technological innovation, and rich cultural experiences. Here are the reasons to study in South Korea:

World-Class Education

South Korea is home to top-ranking universities like Seoul National University (SNU), Korea University, and Yonsei University, recognized globally for their programs in engineering, business, and technology.

High-Quality Education

The country boasts an education system that emphasizes innovation, research, and practical skills, preparing students for competitive global careers.

Affordable Tuition and Scholarships

Compared to Western countries, South Korea offers affordable tuition fees, with numerous scholarships for international students, including the Global Korea Scholarship (GKS).

Technological Advancement

Known as one of the most technologically advanced countries, South Korea provides an excellent environment for students in fields like IT, AI, and robotics.

Rich Cultural Experience

Experience the blend of modernity and tradition through South Korea’s K-pop, traditional festivals, and historic landmarks.

Career Opportunities

South Korea’s strong economy and global industries create ample opportunities for international graduates, especially in IT, business, and manufacturing sectors.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Admission letter from a South Korea university
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

South Korea universities generally have two main intakes:

  • March (Spring Semester): Main intake
  • September (Fall Semester): Secondary intake

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90).
  • Korean-Taught Programs: TOPIK Level 3–5, depending on the program.

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • A minimum CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an South Korean Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Korean university.
  • Receive a Certificate of Admission (COA) from the university.
  • Submit the visa application with required documents at the Korean Embassy or Consulate.
  • Single-entry visa: $30–$50
  • Multiple-entry visa: $80–$100

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: $3,000–$7,000 per semester.
  • Postgraduate Programs: $4,000–$8,000 per semester.
  • Private Universities: Higher tuition fees than public universities.
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: $500–$1,000 (includes accommodation, food, and transportation)..

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: $200–$500 per month
  • Private apartments: $400–$800 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Work Opportunities:

International graduates can apply for a D-10 Job Seeking Visa, allowing them to stay in South Korea while searching for work. South Korea’s thriving industries, especially in IT, technology, and business, offer excellent job prospects for international students.
