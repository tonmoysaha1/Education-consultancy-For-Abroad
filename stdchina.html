STUDY IN CHINA

Thinking of studying in China from Bangladesh? MASJT is here to guide you! With expertise in education systems and application processes, we ensure you find the best program and university to fulfill your academic and professional dreams.

Why Study in CHINA

China is one of the top destinations for international students, offering a blend of high-quality education, affordable tuition fees, and a culturally immersive experience. Here’s why China is an excellent choice:

World-Class Universities

China is home to prestigious institutions like Tsinghua University, Peking University, and Fudan University, which are globally recognized for their academic excellence and research.

Affordable Tuition and Living Costs

Compared to Western countries, tuition fees and living expenses in China are significantly lower, making it an attractive option for students on a budget.

Extensive Scholarship Opportunities

The Chinese government and universities offer numerous scholarships, such as the Chinese Government Scholarship (CGS), which covers tuition, accommodation, and living expenses.

English-Taught Programs

China offers a wide range of English-taught programs, especially in fields like medicine, engineering, business, and technology.

Rich Cultural Experience

China’s rich history, diverse traditions, and vibrant cities offer students a unique cultural experience. You’ll have the chance to learn Mandarin, one of the most widely spoken languages in the world.

Global Career Opportunities

With its growing influence in the global economy, studying in China can open doors to career opportunities both in China and internationally.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Tier 4 Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

  • September (Main Intake): Most programs
  • March (Secondary Intake): Available for some universities

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90)
  • Chinese-Taught Programs: HSK Level 4–6, depending on the program

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA 4.75 to 5.0 in HSC / Min 2C in A Level to 3A* in A Level.
  • Few Universities require Math course in 12th Grade for Business and Science programs.

Postgraduate – Masters

  • CGPA 2.50+
  • GRE or GMAT is not required for Business, Science, and Engineering programs.
  • For MBA programs at some Universities, students need to have work experience along with GMAT score.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an China Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Chinese university.
  • Obtain a JW201 or JW202 form and an admission letter from the university.
  • Submit your visa application with required documents at the Chinese Embassy or Consulate.
  • Attend a medical examination if required.
  • Single-entry visa: $30–$60 (varies by country).

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: $2,000–$6,000 per year.
  • Postgraduate Programs: $3,000–$10,000 per year.
  • MBBS and Engineering Programs: $5,000–$15,000 per year
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: $300–$600 (includes accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: $150–$400 per month
  • Private apartments: $300–$700 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Opportunities:

China allows international students to apply for internships or work in certain industries after graduation. Graduates in fields like engineering, technology, and business often find employment opportunities in multinational companies or Chinese firms.
