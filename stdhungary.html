Thinking about studying in HUNGARY from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN HUNGARY

Are you planning to study in Hungary from Bangladesh? MASJT can assist you in selecting the ideal program and university in one of Europe’s most charming and affordable destinations. Hungary offers a blend of academic excellence, vibrant culture, and exciting career opportunities for international students.

Why Study in HUNGARY

Hungary is becoming a popular study destination due to its high-quality education system, affordable tuition fees, and rich cultural heritage. Here are the reasons why Hungary is an excellent choice for international students:

Renowned Universities

Hungary is home to prestigious institutions like Eötvös Loránd University, University of Szeged, Budapest University of Technology and Economics, and Semmelweis University, known for medicine, engineering, and the sciences.

Scholarships for International Students

The Stipendium Hungaricum Scholarship Program covers tuition, living expenses, and even travel costs for international students.

English-Taught Programs

Many universities in Hungary offer programs in English, making it accessible for international students without the need for Hungarian language proficiency.

Affordable Education and Scholarships

Hungary offers one of the most affordable higher education systems in Europe, with low tuition fees and a cost of living significantly lower than in Western Europe.

Gateway to Europe

Located in Central Europe, Hungary allows easy access to other European countries, providing students with opportunities to travel and explore the continent.

Cultural and Historic Richness

Hungary’s rich history, vibrant cities like Budapest, and a warm, welcoming culture make studying there a truly enriching experience.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Proof of accommodation in Hungary
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

Hungarian universities generally have two main intakes:

  • September (Main Intake): Most programs
  • February (Secondary Intake): Limited programs available

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90).

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an Hungarian Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a Hungarian university.
  • Submit the visa application at the Hungarian Embassy or Consulate in your country.
  • Provide all required documents, including proof of accommodation and financial means.
  • Approximately €60–€100 Visa fee

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: €2,000–€6,000 per year.
  • Postgraduate Programs: €3,000–€7,000 per year.
  • Medicine and Dentistry Programs: €12,000–€16,000 per year
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: €500–€800 (including accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: €150–€300 per month
  • Private apartments: €350–€600 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Work Opportunities:

After completing your studies, you can apply for a 9-month residence permit to search for jobs or start a business in Hungary. Hungary’s growing economy offers opportunities in fields like IT, healthcare, engineering, and business.
