Introduction of MASJT Education Consultancy

MASJT Education Consultancy is a trusted education consultancy firm dedicated to helping students achieve their maximum potential in education. We provide accurate guidance for students in education and career pathways.

Our Mission and Vision

Mission

To guide every student accurately towards their dream education and career.

Vision

To establish ourselves as a world-class education solution provider and a reliable partner in students’ success.

Objectives and Goals

  • Connecting students with internationally renowned educational institutions.
  • Providing the best platform to enhance skills and knowledge.
  • Offering customized solutions based on students’ requirements.

History

MASJT Education Consultancy was founded in 2015 to address the lack of proper guidance for students pursuing higher education.


Founder MD. Abdulla Al Masud , who himself benefited from international education, envisioned creating a reliable platform for students. Under his leadership, the first office was established in 2015, successfully helping over 182 students secure admissions abroad in its first year.
