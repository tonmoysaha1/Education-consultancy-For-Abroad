How Can We Help You?

At MASJT Education Consultancy , we are here to assist you every step of the way in achieving your educational goals. Our help desk provides prompt support for all inquiries and concerns. Below are the key areas where we can help:

Common Queries

Admission Assistance
  • How do I choose the right university or program?

    • At MASJT Education Consultancy, we help you choose the perfect university or program based on your academic profile, budget, and career aspirations.

  • What documents are required for the admission process?

    • You need academic transcripts, certificates, letters of recommendation, a statement of purpose, a valid passport, and other country-specific requirements.

  • Can you guide me with scholarship applications?

    • Yes, we assist you in identifying suitable scholarships and completing the application process successfully.

Visa Processing
  • What are the requirements for a student visa?

    • Common requirements include a valid passport, admission letter, proof of financial support, visa application form, and other country-specific documents.

  • How long does the visa process take?

    • Visa processing time depends on the country but generally takes 2-8 weeks. We provide regular updates and guidance.

  • What should I do if my visa application is rejected?

    • We analyze the reasons for rejection and guide you through the reapplication process with improved documentation and preparation.

Step-by-Step Assistance

  • Contact us with your query.
  • Our team will review your requirements and respond within 24-48 hours.
  • Get personalized advice and solutions from our experts.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How long does it take to process an admission application?

    • The process varies depending on the university. On average, it takes 4-8 weeks.

  • Do you offer help with post-admission processes ?

    • Yes, we assist with finding student accommodation and other post-admission requirements.

  • What is the cost of your services?

    • Our service charges depend on the type of assistance you need. Contact us for a detailed breakdown.
