Thinking about studying in MALAYSIA from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN MALAYSIA

Are you considering studying in Malaysia from Bangladesh? MASJT is here to guide you through the process of selecting the right program and university in one of Asia’s most affordable and dynamic study destinations. With a focus on academic quality, vibrant culture, and excellent career opportunities, Malaysia is an ideal choice for international students.

Why Study in MALAYSIA

Malaysia has become a top destination for international students due to its high-quality education system, affordable tuition, and welcoming multicultural environment. Here’s why you should consider studying in Malaysia:

Globally Recognized Universities

Malaysia is home to leading institutions like University of Malaya (UM), Universiti Teknologi Malaysia (UTM), and branch campuses of renowned global universities such as Monash University Malaysia and Nottingham University Malaysia.

Multicultural Environment

With a blend of Malay, Chinese, and Indian cultures, Malaysia provides a unique and diverse experience for international students.

English-Taught Programs

Many programs in Malaysia are taught in English, making it an accessible destination for students worldwide.

Affordable Education and Scholarships

Malaysia offers one of the most affordable education systems in the world. Tuition fees and living costs are significantly lower compared to Western countries.

Strong Focus on Innovation and Research

Malaysia invests heavily in education and research, providing students access to modern facilities and cutting-edge technology.

Travel and Exploration

Located in the heart of Southeast Asia, Malaysia offers easy access to neighboring countries and countless opportunities to explore its own stunning landscapes and vibrant cities.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Admission letter from a Malaysian university
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

Malaysian universities generally have two main intakes:

  • February and July (Main Intakes): Most programs
  • October (Secondary Intake): Limited programs

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90).

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an Malaysian Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Malaysian university.
  • Submit a visa application through Education Malaysia Global Services (EMGS).
  • Provide all required documents and pay the visa fee.
  • Student visa costs approximately $50–$100 (depending on the program and nationality).

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: $2,000–$5,000 per year.
  • Postgraduate Programs: $3,000–$7,000 per year.
  • Branch Campuses: $10,000–$15,000 per year.
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: $400–$800 (including accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: $100–$300 per month
  • Private apartments: $200–$600 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Work Opportunities:

International students can apply for a work visa after graduation. Malaysia’s growing industries, particularly in IT, engineering, and business, offer excellent job prospects. Additionally, many multinational companies have their regional offices in Malaysia.
