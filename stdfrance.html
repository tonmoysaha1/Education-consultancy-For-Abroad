STUDY IN FRANCE

Are you planning to study in France from Bangladesh? MASJT is here to assist you! Our experienced consultants and graduates can help you find the perfect program and university for your academic and career aspirations.

Why Study in FRANCE

France is one of the world’s leading destinations for international students, known for its prestigious education system, cultural richness, and welcoming environment. Here’s why you should consider studying in France:

World-Class Education

France is home to prestigious institutions such as Sorbonne University, École Polytechnique, and HEC Paris, which are globally renowned for excellence in fields like business, engineering, arts, and humanities.

Low or No Tuition Fees

French public universities offer affordable tuition fees, even for international students. Additionally, scholarships and funding opportunities are widely available.

English-Taught Programs

Many French universities offer programs taught entirely in English, especially at the postgraduate level, making it accessible for international students.

Rich Cultural and Historical Heritage

Studying in France offers the opportunity to immerse yourself in a rich cultural experience, from historic landmarks like the Eiffel Tower to renowned art museums like the Louvre.

Strong Career Opportunities

France is the sixth-largest economy in the world, offering excellent post-study work opportunities, particularly in industries such as luxury goods, engineering, and technology.

Post-Study Work Visa

France allows international students to apply for a Temporary Residence Permit (APS), enabling them to work for up to two years after graduation.

Diverse Student Life

With over 300,000 international students, France has a vibrant and diverse student community, ensuring a welcoming environment for all.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Proof of English or French language proficiency
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Proof of accommodation in France
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

France universities generally have two main intakes:

  • Fall Intake (September): Main intake
  • Spring Intake (January): Secondary intake

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS 6.0–7.0 or TOEFL equivalent
  • French-Taught Programs: DELF/DALF or TCF with minimum B2 proficiency

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • Minimum GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • A minimum CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an France Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Create an account on Campus France and complete the application process.
  • Obtain your admission letter from a French institution.
  • Submit the visa application with the required documents.
  • Attend an interview at the French Embassy or Consulate.
  • Approximately €50–€99

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Public Universities: €170–€650 per year (depending on the program and level).
  • Grandes Écoles or Specialized Programs: €3,000–€10,000 per year.
    Living Expenses:
  • Monthly cost: €800–€1,200 (varies by city).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: €200–€400 per month
  • Private apartments: €500–€800 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Opportunities:

After completing your studies, you may apply for a Temporary Residence Permit (APS), allowing you to work in France for up to two years. Graduates of French institutions are highly sought after in industries like engineering, fashion, business, and IT.
