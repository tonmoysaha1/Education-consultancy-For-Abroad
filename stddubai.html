Study in DUBAI

Thinking about studying in DUBAI from Bangladesh?

STUDY IN DUBAI

Are you considering studying in Dubai from Bangladesh? MASJT is here to help you find the right program and university in one of the world's most dynamic and innovative cities. With our support, you can confidently embark on a journey to academic and career success in Dubai.

Why Study in DUBAI

Dubai has emerged as a global hub for education, innovation, and business. Here are the top reasons to choose Dubai as your study destination:

World-Class Education

Dubai hosts internationally recognized institutions such as University of Dubai, American University in Dubai (AUD), and branch campuses of renowned universities like Heriot-Watt University and University of Birmingham.

Global Exposure

As a cosmopolitan city, Dubai offers students exposure to diverse cultures, languages, and business practices, providing a global perspective on education and career.

English-Taught Programs

Most programs in Dubai are taught in English, making it easier for international students to adapt and excel.

Affordable Education and Scholarships

Dubai offers quality education at competitive tuition rates compared to Western countries. Numerous universities provide scholarships and financial aid for international students.

Thriving Economy and Career Opportunities

Dubai’s booming economy and its status as a global business hub provide excellent job opportunities for graduates, especially in industries like finance, technology, and hospitality.

Safe and Modern Lifestyle

Dubai is one of the safest cities in the world, with world-class infrastructure, healthcare, and entertainment options.

Required Documents

Documents required for University Application:

  • Scan copy of your passport
  • Scan copies (original) of all educational Certificates & Marksheets
  • Scan copies (original) of Two Academic Reference Letters (For PG)
  • Statement of Purpose
  • Scan copy (original) of UKVI/Academic IELTS Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g., extracurricular activities)

Documents required for Visa Application:

  • A valid Passport
  • A completed Online Visa Application Form
  • Appropriate Visa Application Fee
  • Immigration Health Surcharge (IHS)
  • Confirmation of Acceptance for Studies (CAS) Letter from your sponsor (University)
  • Medical Test - IOM
  • Admission letter from a Dubai based university
  • Bank Statement and Bank Solvency Certificate (from Listed Bank only)
  • All educational Certificates and Marksheets (Original)
  • IELTS/TOEFL Certificate
  • Job experience letter (if required)
  • Other supporting documents (if available, e.g extracurricular activities)

Application Procedures

Intakes

Dubai universities generally have two main intakes:

  • September (Fall Semester): Main intake
  • January (Spring Semester): Secondary intake
  • May/June (Summer Semester): Limited programs

Language Requirements

  • English-Taught Programs: IELTS (5.5–6.5) or TOEFL iBT (70–90).

Academic Requirement

Undergraduate – Bachelors

  • GPA of 3.0 (out of 4.0) in high school
  • strong performance in relevant subjects

Postgraduate – Masters

    • CGPA of 2.5–3.0 for most programs.
    • Specific programs may require entrance exams or interviews.

Visa Procedure and Fees

To apply for an Dubai Student Visa , you must provide:

  • Secure admission to a recognized Dubai university.
  • Submit the visa application through the university or directly at the General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs (GDRFA).
  • Provide required documents and pay the visa fees.
  • Student visa costs approximately $1,000–$1,500 (including medical tests and processing fees).

Tuition Fees and Living Costs:
    Tuition Fees:
  • Undergraduate Programs: $10,000–$20,000 per year.
  • Postgraduate Programs: $15,000–$30,000 per year.
    Living Expenses:
  • Estimated monthly cost: $1,000–$1,500 (includes accommodation, food, and transportation).

Accommodation Options:

  • University dormitories: $500–$800 per month
  • Private apartments: $800–$1500 per month

Visa Application Processing Time for Bangladesh:

  • Normal Processing: 8–12 weeks.
  • Priority Processing: Available at an additional fee for expedited processing.

Post-Graduation Work Opportunities:

International students can stay in Dubai to work after graduation by applying for a work visa or the Golden Visa for skilled professionals. Dubai’s thriving job market offers excellent opportunities in industries like IT, finance, tourism, and real estate.
